Watauga River Level Predictor

Created by Noah Bodin

This site uses a Neural Network in coordination with the weather forecast to generate a 10-day prediction of levels (daily average) to expect on the Watauga River Gorge. Sections IV and V are the primary targets of this site.

Updates daily at 8:00am Eastern Time.
